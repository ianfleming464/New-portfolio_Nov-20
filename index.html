Who am I?

Full-stack web developer based in Munich

I'm a certified web developer with a background in intellectual property and management. So why the career change? After years as a manager of one of Ireland's most successful independent bookshops - a position which gave me a voracious appetite for reading and a love of people - my wife and I moved to Germany to seek out new adventures.

Since arriving, we have had two beautiful daughters, eaten an obscene quantity of Brezn, drank a LOT of Augustiner, and had a whole lot of laughs. Fun times. What better new adventure could there be, than learning how to code and jumping head-first into a new career in development?

My studies have taught me that I love to build clean, responsive web applications. I combine my new skills with proven talents, and bring a meticulousness and attention-to-detail to my work, matched only by my dedication and perseverance. When I'm not tinkering with React or juggling with Javascript, my passion is making and performing music. I've played shows in countries as diverse as Latvia, Sardinia, Poland, Ireland, and more. I also love to run, to read (generally not simultaneously) and to savour the wonderful combination of my wife, pizza and wine.
